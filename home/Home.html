About Us

Syntegrate Teleinfra LLP (STLLP), formerly known as MK Enterprises, is one of India's most respected names in passive telecom infrastructure development. Our core operations span Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane, where we partner with national telecom providers to create robust, scalable, and high-performance networks.
We believe in building beyond infrastructure—we build connections, communities, and capacity for the future. Our commitment to excellence and innovation drives us to deliver solutions that not only meet today's demands but anticipate tomorrow's challenges.
