About Us

Company Overview

Syntegrate Teleinfra LLP (STLLP), formerly known as MK Enterprises, is one of India’s most respected names in passive telecom infrastructure development. Our core operations span Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane, where we partner with national telecom providers to create robust, scalable, and high-performance networks.

We believe in building beyond infrastructure—we build connections, communities, and capacity for the future.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, reliable, and sustainable telecom, electric, and gas infrastructure solutions that power connectivity and progress. We aim to bridge urban and rural infrastructure gaps with cutting-edge technology and unyielding service quality.

Our Vision

To become a national leader in smart infrastructure development, enabling India's digital and utility ecosystem to thrive through intelligent, integrated, and future-ready networks.
