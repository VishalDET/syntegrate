Services

Syntegrate Teleinfra LLP provides comprehensive infrastructure services with a focus on end-to-end Optical Fibre Network (OFC) roll-outs, ensuring seamless connectivity from planning to execution.


We specialize in the operation and maintenance of OFC networks, offering reliable performance and long-term support.


Our expertise extends to smart city projects, delivering OFC infrastructure that supports modern, connected urban environments. We also execute FTTx rollouts, enabling high-speed fibre connectivity for homes and enterprises. In addition, we are engaged in gas and electrical infrastructure works, delivering integrated utility solutions that power smart and sustainable growth.
